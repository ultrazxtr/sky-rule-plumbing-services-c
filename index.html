What we fix

Core services

From a dripping tap to a full house re-piping, our crew carries the tools and the parts to close the job in one visit wherever possible.

01

Leak Detection & Repair

Hidden slab leaks, burst pipes and dripping joints traced and sealed before they run up your water bill or damage walls.

02

Pipe Installation & Replacement

New builds and renovations fitted with durable PPR and PVC pipework, sized and routed to code.

03

Drainage & Sewer Lines

Blocked drains, slow sinks and soakaway or septic line issues cleared with rodding and jetting equipment.

04

Water Heaters

Supply, installation, and repair of instant and storage water heaters, correctly earthed and pressure-tested.

05

Boreholes & Water Pumps

Submersible and surface pump installation, pressure tank setup, and troubleshooting for weak or failed water supply.

06

Bathroom & Kitchen Fittings

WC, sink, shower mixer and tap fitting for new installs or upgrades, finished neatly and tested before we leave.
