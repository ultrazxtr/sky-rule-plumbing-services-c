Built on Ring Road 2, working across Abuja

SkyRule Plumbing Services started as a local call-out crew in Galadimawa and grew on word of mouth from neighbours who needed a plumber they could call back.

Our story

A neighbourhood plumber that answers the phone

We're based by CES/Celestine Church on Ring Road 2 in Galadimawa Village, and most of our work still starts with a phone call from someone nearby who needs a leak stopped or a fitting replaced.

That local footing is why we quote honestly: repeat calls and referrals only keep coming if the last job held up. We keep the same small crew on repairs from quote to completion, so there's no handoff and no guessing what was agreed.
